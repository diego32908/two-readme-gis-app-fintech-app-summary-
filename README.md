# Financial Intelligence App

## Overview

This project started as a simple cash flow analyzer and is evolving into a broader financial intelligence platform for both small businesses and individuals.

---

## Old Version (Current MVP)

The current app is a cash flow analyzer:

- Upload a CSV with transactions
- Cleans and standardizes data
- Separates income vs expenses
- Groups data by month
- Calculates:
  - Total income
  - Total expenses
  - Net cash flow
- Shows top spending categories
- Displays recent transactions

Purpose:
Give users a clean dashboard without needing Excel.

---

## New Direction (Expansion)

The app is expanding to support real-world workflows, especially for small businesses.

### Problem:
- Small businesses track finances in notebooks or messy records
- No time to convert everything into spreadsheets

### Solution:
- Upload anything (CSV, PDF, photos of notes)
- System digitizes and structures the data
- Tracks data over time
- Uses AI to generate insights

---

## Key Capabilities (Planned)

### Business Side:
- Digitize notebook records
- Track revenue and expenses over time
- Identify:
  - unnecessary costs
  - low-performing products
- Suggest:
  - cost cuts
  - pricing improvements
  - reinvestment strategies

### Individual Side:
- Analyze personal finances
- Detect wasteful spending
- Suggest ways to save and grow money

Upload → Analyze → Insight → Adjust → Repeat
