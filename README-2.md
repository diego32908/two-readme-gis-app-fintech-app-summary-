# VibeChecker — Vote With Your Wallet

## Overview

VibeChecker is a GIS-based discovery prototype that helps users find businesses based on community signals, preferences, and local civic context.

Instead of just ratings and reviews, users explore businesses through how communities actually experience them — spatially.

Pilot region: Los Angeles.

---

## V1 — What’s Built

Working proof-of-concept map:

- Interactive map (Leaflet)
- 25 fictional businesses across LA
- Each includes:
  - category
  - badges
  - community signal summary:
    - “a few reports”
    - “multiple reports”
    - “majority of reports”
- Civic GIS layer (toggle):
  - Spanish-speaking households %
  - median income
- Clickable markers + area overlays

Purpose:
Show how business data + community signals + civic GIS layers work together spatially.

## Future Scope

Expanding beyond traditional storefronts.

### New Layers

#### Small / Informal Businesses
- swap meets
- yard sales
- local vendors

#### Distributed Inventory Sellers
- storage-based sellers
- small resellers
- side-hustle businesses

These represent economic activity that is not well mapped today.

---

## Long-Term Direction

Not trying to compete with Amazon.

Goal:
- discovery first
- visibility for underrepresented businesses
- simple tools for selling (future)

---

## Core Idea

“Vote with your wallet”

Users discover businesses aligned with their preferences and community signals, then choose where to spend.
